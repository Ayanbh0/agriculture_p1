Potassium Chloride (Muriate of Potash)

Detials:

Types:

- Potassium Fertilizer.

Nutrient Composition:

- Approximately 60% potassium (K2O).

Uses:

- Improves fruit quality, disease resistance, and overall plant vigor.

- Commonly used for fruit trees, vegetables, and grains.

- Helps in water regulation and enzyme activation in plants.
