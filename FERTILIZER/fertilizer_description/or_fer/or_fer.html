ORGANIC FERTOLIZER

Detials:

Types:

- Bone Meal, Blood Meal, Fish Emulsion.

Nutrient Composition:

Varies by type, which are followings -

Bone Meal:

- High in phosphorus (around 15% P2O5).

Blood Meal:

- Rich in nitrogen (around 12-15% nitrogen).

Fish Emulsion:

- Balanced nutrient profile with nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium.

Uses:

- Improve soil health and structure.

- Provide a slow release of nutrients.

- Enhance microbial activity in the soil.
