Magnesium Sulfate (Epsom Salt)

Detials:

Types:

- Micronutrient Fertilizer.

Nutrient Composition:

- Provides magnesium and sulfur.

Uses:

- Essential for chlorophyll production and overall plant health.

- Commonly used for roses, tomatoes, and peppers.

- Helps improve seed germination and nutrient uptake.
